Ten O'Clock company profile

    Ten O’Clock IT Consultancy, established in 2019, is an E-business consultancy service for coffee shops. Our main goal is to assist new or existing coffee businesses to a long-term sustainable. Using our extensive knowledge on IT to help develop and design your coffee website and also manage your information with database storage system. Ten O’Clock IT Consultancy can help you create the best coffee café website that still reflects the identity and the culture of the coffee brand. We can also guide the coffee café to a better understanding in managing their information and improve their stocking system to satisfy their customers’ needs. By helping set the coffee business foundation and integrating its own concept and value, Ten O’Clock IT Consultancy will help you create a comprehensive and unique coffee website.
